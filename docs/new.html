andreas gajdosik

Born 1992, based in Prague. Is an artist and software developer. He focuses on useful art, socially engaged projects and artivism which often incorporate programming, new technologies and interventional provocative attitudes. He is interested in ethical source software, sharing, DIY culture and experimental forms. Was member of dissolved Pavel Ondračka collective. Award holder of Jindrich Chalupecky Award 2019.
