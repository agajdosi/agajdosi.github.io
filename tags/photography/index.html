As I Was Seen

1 September 2014 | artwork > #photography #surveiallance

As I was seen (2014) is a series of 8 auto-portraits with use of traffic cameras around Brno. With camera refresh rate of 1 shot per minute and 2 to 3 minutes delay before shot is available online the photo shoot quickly turned into a strange public ritual.

One Hour of Work

1 May 2014 | artwork > #photography #performance #capital

One Hour of Work (2014) is series of 3 photographs. The photographs do not only refer working author or his graphical card on visual basis but also on capitalist level: earned money were used for printing of the actual pictures. Thus the more earning work, the bigger photography.
