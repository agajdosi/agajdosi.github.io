andreas gajdosik

Born 1992, based in Brno. Is an artist and coder. He focuses on useful art, socially engaged projects and artivism which often incorporate programming, new technologies and interventional provocative attitudes. He is interested in open source, sharing, DIY culture and experimental forms. Is member of experiemental band European Union, was member of dissolved Pavel Ondračka collective. In 2019 he was nominated for Jindrich Chalupecky Award.
